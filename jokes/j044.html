American Jokes 44






A young man proposed marriage to his sweetheart. The girl replied,

"If I marry you, will you promise to give up smoking?"

"Yes, I will", came the reply.

"And drinking?"

"I will give up drinking as well."

"And going to the club with your cronies?"

"Yes, I will."

"And what else will you give up for my sake?" She asked finally.

"I have already given up the idea of marring you !!"
