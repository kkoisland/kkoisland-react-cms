American Jokes 56






A young man came to interview a bank president.

"Tell me, Sir, how did you become so successful?"

"Two words."

"And what are they, Sir?"

"Right decisions."

"How do you make right decisions?"

"One word...Experience."

"And how do you get experience?"

"Two words."

"And what are they?"

"Wrong decisions!"
